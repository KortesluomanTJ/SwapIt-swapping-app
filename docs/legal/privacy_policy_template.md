# SwapIt Privacy Policy (Template, Not Legal Advice)

_Last updated: [DATE]_

SwapIt collects and processes limited personal data to operate a swap-only marketplace.

## What We Collect
- Account data: email, authentication identifiers, optional display name and avatar.
- Verification data: phone number and phone verification metadata.
- Marketplace data: listings, images, offer/counter data, chat messages, reports/blocks.
- Location data: session-based approximate location (country/region/city and protected coordinates) for distance ranking and safety.
- Usage and security logs for abuse prevention.

## Why We Process Data
- Provide core marketplace features (listing, discovery, offers, messaging).
- Enforce safety and anti-abuse controls.
- Improve app performance and reliability.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## Privacy Controls
- Exact coordinates and addresses are never publicly displayed.
- Public listing views show rough meetup labels and rounded distance only.
- Giveaways are opt-in.
- Users can request account deletion.

## Data Retention
- Active account data retained while account exists.
- Deleted account data removed or anonymized per operational and legal needs.
- Moderation/security records may be retained longer where required.

## Your Rights
Depending on your region, you may request access, correction, deletion, and objection to certain processing.
